Brownie

Itens you need

  • Butter
  • Chocolate
  • Sugar
  • Flower
  • Egg(s)
  • Salt
  • A pot
  • A spoon

Ingredients

    To make a 400g Brownie, which will serve 8-10 portions, you will need:

  • 125g of butter
  • 125g of chocolate
  • 100g of sugar
  • 50g flower
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 spoon of salt (1,5g)

Observations

The semisweet chocolate on this recipe must be 50% Cocoa and 50% Sugar

The best version of this recipe uses a 25% cocoa and 75% sugar ratio, thus you could use pure cocoa with sugar or sweeter chocolates with less sugar, for a precise amount use the calculator below:
