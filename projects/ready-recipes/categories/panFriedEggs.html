Pan Fried Eggs

Itens you need

  • Egg(s)
  • A Pan
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Method

  1. Put arround 2g of butter for each egg in the pan while it's still cold
  2. Turn on the heat on the lowest option and wait for the butter to melt completelly, try not to get the pan too hot
  3. If you want a more presentable eggs, you should add the salt and pepper below the eggs
  4. Add the Eggs with care to not break the Yolk
  5. Don't touch the egg until there's a layer of white totally cooked, after this, check if the eggs is not stick
  6. If the egg is sticked, try to loosen it by the sides first, then the yolk
  7. The perfect fried egg is when the white is almost totally cooked, but the yolk is still liquid
